Welcome to my SIP Innovation Project. This initiative was created to address the unique challenges families and individuals with special needs face when it comes to safety, communication, and emotional support. My focus is on developing solutions—like the Interactive Sensory Plant—that combine security with sensory comfort, creating tools that are both practical and compassionate.

This project is more than just a concept—it’s a step toward inclusive innovation designed by and for the community. By blending technology with lived experience, the goal is to create solutions that not only protect but also empower. Thank you for exploring this work, and I invite you to imagine what’s possible when safety, accessibility, and emotional connection come together.
